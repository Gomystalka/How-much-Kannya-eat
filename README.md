# How-much-Kannya-eat
<h1>How much Kannya eat?</h1>
<h2><b>MANY FEATURES FROM THE ORIGINAL GDD WERE NOT IMPLEMENTED DUE TO TIME LIMITATIONS</b></h2>

<p>This will be a simple <b>Collect-a-thon</b> around a procedurally generated city</p>
<p>This project is being made for educational purposes only.</p>
<p>Any credits will be made within the game and on this repository</p>

<h1>Objective</h1>
Your Objective is to keep your Hunger Bar up by devouring Crabs.
There are four different Crab Rarities. The rarity determines how much hunger the crab replenishes.
<ul>
  <li><b>Common -> Gray</b></li>
  <li><b>Rare -> Green</b></li>
  <li><b>Super Rare -> Blue</b></li>
  <li><b>Ultra Rare -> Orange</b></li>
</ul>
To eat a Crab you have caught, go into your Inventory and click on the Crab you wish to eat.

<h1>Controls</h1>
<ul>
  <li><b>RMB/Q -> Catch</b></li>
  <li><b>TAB -> Inventory</b></li>
  <li><b>SPACE -> Jump</b></li>
  <li><b>WSAD -> Movement</b></li>
  <li><b>Mouse -> Camera Movement</b></li>
  <li><b>Scroll Wheel -> Camera Zoom</b></li>
</ul>
